Using a pen, draw a square in the center of the paper.
Locate the middle of the square’s top line and draw a small circle slightly above the line.
Draw a vertical line to connect the circle to the top of the square.
Draw two equally spaced vertical lines from the square’s bottom line.
Draw a short horizontal line connected to the middle of the square’s left side line.
Repeat on the right side of the square.
Draw two dots and a curved line to form a smiley face inside the square.
